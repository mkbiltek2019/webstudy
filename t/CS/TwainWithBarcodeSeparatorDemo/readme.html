TWAIN With Barcode Separator Demo

Demonstrates TWAIN scanning and barcode separation feature.

  • Scans pages from a TWAIN source and saves the acquired pages to multi-page TIFF files.

    Uses the LEADTOOLS barcode engine to search for Code128 barcodes on the pages.

    If a user-specified barcode string is detected, the separator is saved to a new TIFF file, which is appended with all subsequent pages until a new separator is detected.
